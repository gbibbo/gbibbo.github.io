Short Bio

Mr. Ziwei Liu is currently a Ph.D. candidate in Multimedia Lab, Department of Information Engineering, CUHK. He is supervised by Prof. Xiaoou Tang and Prof. Xiaogang Wang. His research interests include computational photography, computer vision and machine learning.

Ziwei graduated from the Advanced Class, Huazhong University of Science and Technology with a B.Eng. in 2013. He is also fortunate to have internships at Microsoft Research and Google Research.
