Short Bio

Mr. Xubo Liu is a Ph. D. candidate from 2020 - 2024 (expected) at the Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing (CVSSP), University of Surrey supervised by Prof. Wenwu Wang and Prof. Mark Plumbley. His current research interests include audio-visual learning, signal processing and machine learning.

Xubo received his B. Eng. in Telecommunications Engineering (2016 - 2020) with First Class Honors from Queen Mary University of London (QMUL) and Beijing Univ. of Posts & Telco. (BUPT). Previously, he was a research assistant at CSLT, Tsinghua University and a software engineer intern at IBM Research.
