Short Bio

Mr. Xubo Liu is a 1st year Ph. D. student at the Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing (CVSSP), University of Surrey under the supervision of Prof. Wenwu Wang and Prof. Mark Plumbley. His current research interests include neural audio generation and multi-modal machine learning.

Xubo received the B. Eng. with First Class Honors from Queen Mary University of London (QMUL) and Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in July 2020. Previously, he was a research assistant at CSLT, Tsinghua University and a software engineer intern at IBM Research.
