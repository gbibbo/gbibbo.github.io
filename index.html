Short Bio

Dr. Ziwei Liu is currently a post-doctoral research fellow (2017-) in UC Berkeley / ICSI, working with Prof. Stella Yu. His research interests include computer vision, learning and computational photography.

Ziwei received his Ph.D. (2013-2017) from CUHK / Multimedia Lab, advised by Prof. Xiaoou Tang and Prof. Xiaogang Wang. He is fortunate to have internships at Microsoft Research and Google Research.
